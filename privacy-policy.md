# Privacy Policy for Rebo - Reviews for Renters

  Last updated: November 18, 2024

  ## Data We Collect
  - Email address (for account creation)
  - Location data (to show nearby properties)
  - User-generated content (reviews, photos, ratings)
  - Profile information (display name, profile photo)

  ## How We Use Data
  - To provide rental property reviews and ratings
  - To show properties near your location
  - To enable user authentication and account management
  - To moderate user-generated content

  ## Data Sharing
  We do not sell your personal information. User reviews are public and visible to all app users.

  ## Data Storage
  Your data is stored securely using Firebase/Firestore with encryption.

  ## Your Rights
  You can request deletion of your account and data by contacting: soccer10ns@gmail.com

  ## Contact
  For privacy questions, email: soccer10ns@gmail.com
